Physician's FAQ

Is this just a standard volume-filling procedure?

No. While filler is used, the primary objective is structural harmony and focal balance, rather than sheer volume expansion. We use precise amounts to define transitions and support facial architecture.

Will it look artificial or "overfilled"?

Our philosophy fundamentally opposes the artificial look. By treating the face as a composition, respecting natural anchor points, and preserving dynamic movement, the result is highly natural and proportioned.

Can I combine this with deep lifting devices like Ultherapy?

Yes. Pairing deep tissue tension (via Ultherapy) with structural contouring (Volume Chamaka-se) provides an optimal comprehensive reset. This combination addresses both gravity and harmony simultaneously.

How much filler (cc) will I need?

For most patients seeking harmony rather than extreme volume, the exact amount is strictly determined by the physician after assessing your specific anatomy, avoiding unnecessary over-treatment.
