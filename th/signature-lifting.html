Is it genuine Ultherapy?

Absolutely. We use only authentic Merz Ultherapy devices and transducers. We unseal the new tip in front of you before the procedure, and you can verify the authenticity QR code immediately.

Does it hurt?

Ultherapy delivers heat deep into the tissue, which can cause a prickly or aching sensation. While many patients tolerate it well with topical numbing, we employ a tiered comfort protocol.

For sensitive areas, we offer บล็อกเฉพาะจุดเพื่อความสบาย (ทางเลือกเพิ่ม +₩50,000) to improve comfort based on anatomy. For maximum relaxation, we offer IV sedation แบบรู้สึกตัว (ทางเลือกเพิ่ม +₩100,000).

Can I combine this with injectables?

Yes. Combining Ultherapy (structure) with injectables (volume/texture) yields the best "Chamaka-se" result. We typically perform energy devices first, followed by injectables in the same session.
