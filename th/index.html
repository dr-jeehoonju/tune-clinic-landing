How much downtime should I expect?

ระยะพักฟื้น depends on the pathway selected:

  • ยกกระชับing: Minimal visible downtime
  • Juvelook: Mild swelling may occur
  • Filler: Bruising is possible, especially in delicate areas

We prioritize structured planning to minimize unnecessary recovery time for travelers.

Is pain management available for lifting procedures?

Yes. We offer บล็อกเฉพาะจุดเพื่อความสบายs and IV conscious sedation (optional) to optimize comfort.

Eligibility is confirmed after reviewing your medical history on the day of treatment.

How do I choose the right program?

You don't need to. Chamaka-se is a physician-curated model.

During consultation, we assess:

  • Tissue thickness
  • วอลุ่ม distribution
  • Skin laxity
  • Travel timeline

The goal is not to offer more options - but to identify the most rational pathway for you.

How do I prepare before my visit?

To optimize your consultation, please prepare:

  • A front-facing photo in natural light
  • Your travel dates
  • Your primary concern (lift / volume / collagen support)

We recommend booking in advance due to limited daily appointment volume.
