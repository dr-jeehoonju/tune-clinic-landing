What this protocol actually is

Autologous regenerative activation, then a physician-selected energy device.

Metacell Protocol starts with your own biology. We draw your blood, separate the platelet-rich plasma, and activate the platelets with PBM (photobiomodulation, also known as low-level light therapy). The result is an autologous concentrate of your own growth factors and signaling proteins, prepared in our clinic in under an hour, ready to be delivered into the layers of your face that need them.

We deliberately do not use the imprecise phrase that's often advertised next to autologous regenerative care. There is no banked donor cell line in this protocol, no proprietary culture, no “youth serum” sourced from outside your body. What we do is autologous regenerative activation: your platelets, your growth factors, activated through PBM, delivered into the same face that produced them.

The second half of the protocol is the energy device the physician selects for your face. Some patients need deep structural lift — Ultherapy reaches the SMAS layer. Some need surface tightening across a wider surface area — Thermage heats dermal collagen. Many patients need both, balanced. The Metacell layer amplifies recovery and biological response across whichever device the physician chooses, which is why the final price varies: it scales with the depth and surface area of the lift you actually need.

Physician's FAQ

Is this the same as the “cell therapy” often advertised?

No. There is no banked donor cell line and no cultured cell product in this protocol. Metacell Protocol is autologous regenerative activation: your platelet-rich plasma is prepared from your own blood and activated using PBM (photobiomodulation), then delivered into your face. We deliberately avoid the more dramatic word that's often advertised because it implies something this protocol does not include.

How long do results last?

The regenerative layer (Metacell) drives a recovery and quality boost that typically peaks at 8–12 weeks and remains visible for several months. The energy-device layer (Ultherapy / Thermage) is the part that produces structural lift, with collagen remodeling that consolidates over 3–6 months and lifts that are typically discussed in the 12–18 month range. Maintenance schedules are individualized at follow-up.

Is this safe for short-stay travel timelines?

Yes, when the build is matched to your trip. A Metacell-only or Metacell + lighter energy build can comfortably fit in a 3–5 day Seoul trip. A deeper combination build (Ultherapy 350 + Thermage 600 + Metacell) typically wants a 4–5 day window so the physician can see you for a follow-up before you fly. The decision is made at consultation, not before.

Why isn't the price fixed?

Because the energy-device layer changes per face. The Metacell baseline is fixed at ₩2,000,000. The Ultherapy / Thermage layer scales with shot count, depth, and which device the physician chooses, so the final total realistically lands anywhere from ₩2,000,000 (Metacell-only) up to roughly ₩4,000,000 (deepest combination build). You see the itemized KRW total before you commit.

Can I do this on a 3-day trip?

Often, yes — for a Metacell + lighter energy build. For the deeper combination builds we typically prefer a 4–5 day window so we can see you again before departure. If your trip is fixed at three days, tell us at consultation and we will design the build around the time you actually have, not around the protocol that would over-treat for your window.

Is sedation included?

Topical anesthetic and a complimentary targeted comfort block are standard. IV conscious sedation is optional (+₩110,000) and most often selected when the build includes a deeper Ultherapy + Thermage combination. The physician will recommend at consultation whether sedation is worthwhile for your specific build.
