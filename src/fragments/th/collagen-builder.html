Physician's FAQ

Will this make me look "overfilled"?

No. Rejuran Black and Juvelook Skin are biostimulators, not traditional structural fillers. They are placed superficially to improve dermal density, elasticity, and texture, ensuring a completely natural progression rather than volume distortion.

How soon will I see changes?

Initial improvements in hydration and texture can often be felt within 1–2 weeks as the tissue begins to repair. However, peak collagen density and fine-line reduction typically materialize between 4 to 8 weeks.

What if I have an event soon?

Because these are superficial injections, you may experience temporary micro-bumps or mild swelling for 24–48 hours. We strongly recommend allowing a 2-to-3 day buffer before any major social events or professional photoshoots.

Can I combine this with lifting?

Yes. Combining energy-based lifting (which targets deep tissue structure) with biostimulators (which target surface density and texture) is highly effective and frequently performed in the same session.

How is the protocol customized?

During the consultation, the physician maps your face to identify areas of thinning skin, enlarged pores, or fine lines. Dosage concentration and injection depth are dynamically adjusted to target these specific anatomical requirements.
