FAQ

Hair loss and scalp treatment questions

Can I start hair or scalp treatment during a short trip to Seoul?
Often yes, especially for evaluation and supportive regenerative care. The more important question is whether the goal is maintenance, scalp support, or a more comprehensive long-term strategy.
Can one treatment solve every kind of hair loss?
No. Pattern, timing, scalp condition, and progression matter. That is why the first decision is diagnosis and category fit, not just choosing a popular name.
Is regenerative scalp care the same as a hair transplant?
No. Regenerative scalp support and transplant logic belong to different conversations. One centers on tissue environment and support, while the other addresses hair restoration more directly.
