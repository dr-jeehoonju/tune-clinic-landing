Physician's FAQ

Why combine two energy devices in one session?

Each technology targets a different structural layer. Ultherapy 300 shots reach the deepest tissue (SMAS), while Thermage 600 shots tighten the dermal collagen layer. Together, they produce a synergistic result that no single device can achieve alone. IV sedation ensures complete comfort throughout.

Is this protocol safe in a single session?

Yes. The energy devices operate at different tissue depths and wavelengths, meaning they do not compete or overlap. The physician sequences the protocol to maximize safety and efficacy. IV sedation ensures you remain comfortable throughout the extended session.

How does downtime compare to a single procedure?

Because we are addressing multiple layers with two powerful devices, expect slightly more swelling than a single-device treatment. Most patients see significant subsidence within 3–5 days. IV sedation is included as standard to ensure maximum comfort during the extended session.

Can I do a lighter version first?

Absolutely. If you're unsure, starting with the Signature Lifting (Ultherapy only) is a great entry point. You can always upgrade to the full Structural Reset with Thermage in a follow-up visit.
